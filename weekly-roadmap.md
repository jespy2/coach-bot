- `effort:S` — review, study, learn, quiz, read (≈1h)
- `effort:M` — setup, configure, integrate, build, implement, dockerize (≈2–3h)
- `effort:L` — k8s/helm/terraform/deploy/production/exam, and all **week 8** capstone work (≈4–6h)
